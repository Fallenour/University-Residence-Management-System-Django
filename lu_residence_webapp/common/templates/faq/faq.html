    1. What is the minimum time (months) I need to register for?
      -> 2 Terms (8 months) for undergraduates and 3 terms (12 months) for graduates.

    2. Can I my partner who is not lakehead student live with me at the residence?
      -> No, You can't. Only lakehead students are allowed to live permanently.
    3. Does the residence have laundry facility?
      -> Yes, it has with plenty of machines.

    4. Do you initially support us in buying grocery?
      -> Yes, we provide service once a week for the first month.
    5. Can I leave the residence mid-way?
      -> You can but you will loose your deposit. So, it is highly discouraged to do so.

    6. Does the residence give any discount for needy student?
      -> Yes, it has. It is case by case.
    7. Is the residence multicultural?
      -> Yes, our residence is highly multicultural.

    8. Can I pray at the residence?
      -> Yes, you can. You just need to make sure that you don't disturb others.
    9. How far is the downtown from the residence?
      -> 10 minutes on drive, 20 min on public transit, 30 min if you walk.
